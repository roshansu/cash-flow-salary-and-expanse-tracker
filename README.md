# Cashflow Dashboard

A simple and interactive **Cashflow Dashboard** built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** that helps you track your **salaries and expenses**. This dashboard allows users to visualize their cash inflows and outflows, helping with personal finance management.

---

## Features

- **Track Income:** Track your expanse month vise
- **Track Income:** Add your salary or any source of income.
- **Track Expenses:** Log your daily, weekly, or monthly expenses.
- **Dashboard Overview:** View total income, total expenses, and remaining balance.
- **Responsive Design:** Works well on both desktop and mobile devices.
- **Interactive UI:** Easily add, update, and remove entries.

---

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5** – Structure and layout
- **CSS3** – Styling, including responsive design
- **JavaScript** – Dynamic updates and calculations
- Optional: **LocalStorage** to persist your data (if implemented)
